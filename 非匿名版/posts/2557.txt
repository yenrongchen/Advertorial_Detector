標題：#國外遊記 霸王花生日之旅

30+的生日送給自己世界上最大的花！看到從寶可夢走出來的霸王花，讓我對於這個世界更加的具象化，霸王花雖然有一股腐臭的氣味，但正是這份不討喜，吸引了它需要的昆蟲，完成生命中最重要的使命，人生也是一樣，不必迎合所有人的喜好，你的與眾不同，也許正是成就你的關鍵力量。
For my 30+ birthday, I gifted myself the world’s largest flower! Seeing the Rafflesia, as if it had stepped out of Pokémon, made this world feel even more vivid to me. Though it carries a foul odor, it is precisely this unpleasant scent that draws in the insects it needs to fulfill its most important mission in life. Life is the same—you don’t need to cater to everyone’s tastes. Your uniqueness, even if not always appreciated, may very well be the very force that shapes your greatest achievements.
